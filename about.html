About Us

COVID-19 is raging in India and people are dying because of the sheer lack of oxygen, the supply of which is available, but for which transportation and distribution is the main challenge. There are currently few organized non-profit operations to get medical oxygen to hospitals and patients at home.
‍
In response, Dr. Ramanan Laxminarayan, Founder and Director of the Center for Disease Dynamics, Economics & Policy (CDDEP), put out a call to his network and Oxygen for India was born. The organization is a collective of medical and public health professionals, volunteers, and concerned citizens around the world.
